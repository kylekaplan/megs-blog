and so it begins,

a new paper glove

to store my precious thoughts

...

a snapshot in time

a counselor,

a friend,

a guide,

an extension of me

...

watch me be free

whilst guarding my scribbles & scrambles

with your hefty spine

...

thanks for being

a loyal companion of mine.
