![](/images/uploads/firsthaiku.jpeg)

emblazoned branches\
reminding me of my brain,\
firing new pathways

cerebral escapes\
embarked on through dream and word,\
my great odyssey

illuminating\
the subconscious recesses\
i am coming home.
